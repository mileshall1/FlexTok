"Like My Cheese Drippy, Bruh" is a viral quote from Logan Paul in a September 2024 video featuring KSI and MrBeast comparing their Lunchly product to Lunchables. While dipping nachos, Paul praises Lunchly's cheese for its "drip." The clip gained traction on YouTube and TikTok, inspiring memes and AI-edited versions replacing Paul with various characters.

The Australian Senator Gen Z Slang Speech (or Fatima Payman Brainrot Speech) refers to a viral mid-September 2024 speech by Australian Senator Fatima Payman, packed with Gen Z and Gen Alpha slang like "Skibidi Toilet," "Sigma," and "Goofy Ahh." Intended to critique social media age restrictions, the speech sparked widespread debate online about its effectiveness and relevance in Parliament.

Blackbeard Writing (or Pirate Writing) is a One Piece fan art meme featuring Blackbeard using a quill pen on a map. Originally created by DeviantArt artist jen-and-kris in 2012, it became a viral meme in 2023 on Reddit and later spread to TikTok in 2024, mainly through photo slideshows.
