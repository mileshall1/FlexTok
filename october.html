KSI's "Thick Of It" is a 2024 song featuring Trippie Redd that went viral on TikTok in October 2024. Listeners mocked its lyrics, comparing it to generic video game or movie soundtrack music. Memes satirized KSI's business ventures, like Prime Energy and Lunchly, portraying Gen Alpha fans blindly consuming his products. The mockery also tied into KSI, Logan Paul, and MrBeast's feud with Minecraft YouTuber DanTDM, who criticized them for marketing low-quality products to kids.

Ding Dong Eat It Up is a viral prank video of a man cooking butter chicken curry in class while singing, "Ding dong, eat it up. Eat it well and mix it up," mimicking an Indian stereotype. Originally posted by YouTuber Fique in mid-October 2024 as "Cooking Curry During College Lecture!", it went viral on TikTok, leading to remixes, lip dubs, and recreations.
