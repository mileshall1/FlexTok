The "Jonkler" meme is a comedic misspelling of "The Joker," originating from a 2023 Reddit post that humorously asked, "Why does the jonkler? is he stupid?" The meme is known for its intentional grammatical errors and absurd humor, often paired with images or videos of The Joker. In 2024, it evolved into the "Evil Jonkler Cart" trend, featuring AI-generated animations of The Joker with erratic laughter, usually set to remixed or distorted audio, further amplifying its chaotic and surreal appeal.

The "Circle Chain" meme refers to a viral TikTok trend from August 2024, centered around a circular locket with wings, typically used to hold a photo of a deceased loved one. Memes humorously referenced the chain's sentimental meaning, with jokes about needing to "fill" the locket, often implying exaggerated or darkly comedic scenarios. The trend gained traction as users shared skits and videos showcasing their own Circle Chains.
